Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

An extremely easy to make tortilla pizza, light enough to be a snack, or eaten alone!

Ingredients

  • Large Flour Tortilla
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasonings
    • Garlic Powder
    • Black Pepper
    • Dried Oregano
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings
    • Sliced, Cooked Chicken Breast
    • Green Bell Pepper
    • Green Onions
    • Anything else you want!
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil.
  2. Season with the list of seasonings to your tastes.
  3. Top with tomato sauce and your toppings, and sprinkle cheese on top.
  4. Air fry at 400 degrees F until the cheese is melted, usually within 3-5 minutes.
