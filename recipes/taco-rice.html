Taco Rice Skillet

Description

A delicious one-pan dish featuring chicken, black beans, zesty tomatoes and taco seasoning cooked with brown rice and topped with cheese, perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can RoTel diced tomatoes and green chilies, un-drained
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/4 cup instant brown rice, uncooked
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar and monterey jack cheese blend
  • 1 teaspoon chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add chicken and cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Add onion and taco seasoning, cooking for 2 more minutes
  4. Stir in black beans, un-drained tomatoes and water, bring to a boil
  5. Stir in rice
  6. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 7 to 10 minutes, or until rice is tender
  7. Stir in 1/2 cup of cheese
  8. Sprinkle top with remaining cheese and cilantro, if desired
